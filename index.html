Treasurer

Vision:

A continued recovery from the effects of Covid is a must, and through my vision as Treasurer I will establish a strong financial backing for the society whilst instilling a positive ethos among the executive team. My vision for 2021 as treasurer is to support strong propositions from team members that focus on growth and development, whilst ensuring that proposed budgets and monetary plans are not subject to sudden changes. Through improving already formulised methods, CSESoc will be able to ensure the quality of events as well as promoting social and career growth of the student body.

Goals:

- Ensure full transparency of the society's financial position with the executive team; relaying any immediate issues that arise
- Ensure financial viability and future sustainability of the society
- Ensure all financial statements and records are kept in a neat concise manner
- Provide financial backing accurately and proportionally to portfolios to maximise their full potential

Relevant Experience:

- Created ledger statements for a wide asset portfolio inclusive of 4 different properties
- Efficiently ran an AirBnB business for 8 months, generating a profit of over $18,000;
dealt with refunds (due to COVID) and normal transactions
- Competant in using excel to maintain spreadsheets

Arc Delegate

Vision:

My vision as arc delegate for CSESoc is to maintain the close relations with Arc, win grants for many of our flagship events and to be recognised as one of the best societies in UNSW (like in 2019). By ensuring the society gains the maximum amount of resources avaliable, CSESoc will be an environment which allows students to not only develop as individuals but as professionals as well. In addition to promoting CSESoc as a place where students can grow, as a part of the executive team it's important to foster a culture which promotes enthusiasm, commitment and inclusiveness within the society which will set the precedent for directors, subcom and members as a whole.

Goals:

- Establish more online events, allowing for the opportunity of growth
- With the executive team, grow our membership base through catering of different types of events
- Win grants for our flagship events such as Hackathon
- To provide a dependable way to liaise between Arc and the executive team

Relevant Experience:

- Through working simulatenously 3 different jobs across hospitality and education; therefore am able to adapt my communication style to who I'm talking to
- Has knowledge (read up on it) of Arc grants and funding, requirements, possibilities and forms
